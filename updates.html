Indian Updates

200 KLPD corn/grain-based distillery by Godavari Biorefineries

India bio-economy updates from BioBiz Godavari Biorefineries is investing ₹130 crore to set up a 200-KLPD corn/grain-based distillery in Karnataka with alternate feedstocks. The project supports India's Ethanol Blended Petrol program.

World’s Largest Sustainable Aviation Fuel Plants By TruAlt

ndia bio-economy updates from BioBiz Vijay Nirani, MD of TruAlt Bioenergy, emphasized biofuels as essential for reducing aviation emissions at the India Economic Conclave 2024. TruAlt plans a major sustainable aviation fuel plant using biomass waste and 17-18 Compressed Biogas plants.

Biofuel production from Seaweed Biomass: HPCL partners with Sea6 Energy

India bio-economy updates from BioBiz HPCL and Sea6 Energy signed an MoU to convert seaweed biomass into biofuels and chemicals. The partnership focuses on scaling production and improving seaweed farming. This collaboration aligns with India’s net-zero goals, the National Biofuel Policy.

Green Hydrogen Plant in Baddi: Oil India and Himachal Pradesh Power Corporation partner

India bio-economy updates from BioBiz Greater Noida Authority will establish Bio-CNG plants at Jalpura and Pauvari cow shelters to convert cow dung into fuel. Proceeds from the Bio-CNG sales will support the shelter. Construction at Jalpura is starting soon, while at Pauvari tendering is still ongoing.

Biogas Plant to Be Revived by Faridabad Municipal Corporation

India bio-economy updates from BioBiz Faridabad Municipal Corporation plans to revive its biogas plant, converting organic waste into 250 kg of bio-CNG daily, at a cost of ₹3.50 crore. The plant processes kitchen and green waste to aid waste management. MCF is inviting proposals for managing the plant

Straw-Based Biogas Plant Commercialization

India bio-economy updates from BioBiz Punjab Agricultural University (PAU) partnered with Panj Aab Bio Fuel & Fertilizers to commercialize a paddy straw-based biogas plant using mild steel sheet technology.
